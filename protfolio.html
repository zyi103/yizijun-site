Memo

To: All Employees

Smoke-Free Environment

Fitness Exercise Equipment Inc.

 

02/12/2019

 

Dear Employees,

This notification is provided to all Fitness Exercise Equipment Inc. employees of our new policy in regard to support a Smoke-Free Environment.

Please be informed that in 12th Apr. 2019, all facility of Fitness Exercise Equipment Inc. will be smoke-free. The purpose of a smoke-free policy is to reduce the secondhand smoke indoor and provide a safe and healthy work environment. Smoking is defined as inhaling and exhaling any tobacco product, including e-cigarettes and vaping. This policy applies to all employees, visitors and persons who enter the facilities.

Any activities which violates this new policy will have a fine of 15% of the insurance cost.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Sincerely,

 

 

C.K. Mitchell

C.E.O of Fitness Exercise Equipment Inc.

Unsolicited Recommendation Letter

Feb 11th, 2019
Manager of Theory Syracuse
Peak Point

919 E. Genesee St.
Syracuse, NY 13210


Dear manager,

I am writing to recommend a change in Theory Syracuse. As a resident of this apartment for one and a half semester. I realized a problem with trash removal. I have a mixed feeling about theory, I do understand it’s hard to start and manage a college apartment. I see the team have to spend the effort to manage the dorm environment, but there are still more to do.

Last year, before winter break, there was a problem with the garbage bin outside of the elevator. I realized every time when I took the elevator to the fifth floor, if it’s a Monday, there will be a pile of trash in the trash bin. That smell is unpleasant and hard to describe. The theory made a move to post a note above to remind the resident not to pile their trash in the trash bin. Later, switch the trash bin to a smaller, but a more manageable one. It’s clear to see Theory had spent the effort to make this apartment better.

Now, I want to raise your attention to the garbage disposal around the single elevator shaft. For the last couple of weeks, I found that our resident leaves and pile garbage bags on the floor. I had to admit, I also left my garbage bag on the floor. It’s hard when the door of the garbage shaft was stuck. I have asked many of my friends who also live in Theory. They have to face the same problem. The door was the problem that causes these problems.

I recommend to create a system without the button controls the garbage shaft door, but with a new manual system to keep the trash removal system function smoothly and stable.

I sincerely believe Theory can and have the ability to make the apartment better. I hope you can take my suggestion into consideration. Wish you have the successful year of 2019.

If you have any question, please contact me through zyi103@syr.edu


Best,
Zijun Yi
