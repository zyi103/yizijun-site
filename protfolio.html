I am writing to recommend a change in Theory Syracuse. As a resident of this apartment for one and a half semester. I realized a problem with trash removal. I had to admit, I also left my garbage bag on the floor. It’s hard when the door of the garbage shaft was stuck. I have asked many of my friends who also live in Theory. They have to face the same problem. The door was the problem that causes these problems.

I recommend to create a system without the button controls the garbage shaft door, but with a new manual system to keep the trash removal system function smoothly and stable.

I sincerely believe Theory can and have the ability to make the apartment better. I hope you can take my suggestion into consideration. Wish you have the successful year of 2019.

If you have any question, please contact me through zyi103@syr.edu

Instruction

How to reach the South campus for FREE 

 

Introduction: 

             South Campus is a big part of Syracuse University. There are many facilities on the south campus. To ice skate, you can stop at Skytop & Tennity Ice Rink; order Burger King with SU card, stop at Goldstein Student Center. Many school activities happen in this area as well.  South Campus is home for more than 2,500 students. These instructions will help you find the way to Small, Slocum, Skytop, Winding Ridge and Chinook on South Campus. By the end of the instructions, you should have been able to have found your way to South Campus easily.  

*IMPORTANT NOTE! BUS # IS SU344 NOT SU443. 

 

Description of the Equipment: 

A map is important, it gives you a sense of space and direction. Utilize a map can help you find the way you go more efficiently. On the right side, it is a road map of the SU344 bus route. The link below will show you the bus schedule. 

https://www.centro.org/docs/default-source/schedule-documents/su-schedules/2018-11-05-su-344-south-campus.pdf?sfvrsn=3034f9d_12 

*Be aware this schedule was made on Nov 5. 2018. Schedule might change with new updates.  

 

Material and Equipment:  

  • Some type of map 
  • Internet connection 
  • A device with browser 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Steps: 

  1. Find which bus to take 
  1. Click the link from the description of the equipment section (bus schedule link) 

*  Be aware this schedule was made on Nov 5. 2018. Schedule might change with new updates.  

  1. Navigate to the page says SU-South Campus (Weekday) 
  1. Find the time when the bus departs 

 

 

  1. Find your way to College Place 
  1. Navigate your map and find the College Place. It should be located across Life and Science building. 
  1. The College bus station look like the picture above! 
  1. Find the Information button at the right-hand side if you face the road. 
  1. Hit the button! And look up. 
  1. The board tells you how many mintues left for the SU344 bus to arrive. 

 

 

  1. Take SU344 Bus. All bus that starts with SU are free for all students to take!  

 

 

 

  1. Get off on the Goldstein Student Center  
  1. Pull the yellow string on the window frame when the next stop is your bus stop. 
  1. Push on the yellow strip, to get out of the buses  

 

 

 (End of the Section) 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Troubleshooting: 

Q: Where is College Place? 

A: The College Place is located right across Life and Science Building. Also, you can google College Place on Google Map. 

 

Q: I took the wrong bus, what should I do? 

A: There are many buses leaving from College Place. Sometimes, you might take the wrong bus. It’s ok, ask the bus driver. He will tell you if you should stay on the bus or took another bus. 

 

Q: If found yourself not sure of the bus is going the right direction?  

A: It is, the bus is only going one direction, it loops around between south campus and College Place.   

For more answers, you can ask the bus driver questions, or look up online at centro.org.  

(End of the instructions)
